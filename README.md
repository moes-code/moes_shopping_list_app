A shopping list app for Android that lets you organize your purchases using custom categories.

Organize Your Shopping with Custom Categories – Completely Private & Offline

Shopping List is a simple, yet powerful app that helps you manage your groceries and shopping items efficiently. Create your own custom categories to organize your purchases exactly the way you need them.

Key Features:

✓ Custom Categories – Create and manage personalized categories to organize your shopping lists your way

✓ 100% Offline – No internet connection required. Use the app anytime, anywhere

✓ Complete Privacy – Zero data collection. Your shopping lists stay on your device

✓ Open Source – Published under the MIT License. Transparent, trustworthy, and community-driven

✓ Simple & Intuitive – Clean interface designed for quick and easy shopping list management

✓ Lightweight – Minimal storage space and battery usage

Perfect for:

Weekly grocery shopping
Organizing shopping by store sections
Planning multiple shopping trips
Anyone who values privacy and offline functionality

Open Source Commitment

This app is completely open source and available under the MIT License. You can review the code, contribute, or modify it to suit your needs. We believe in transparency and user privacy.

No Ads. No Tracking. No Account Required.

Just a straightforward shopping list app that does what it's supposed to do – help you shop smarter.
